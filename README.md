## 🌟 Overview

**Trust Layer** is a premium, enterprise-grade product authentication and batch management platform designed to combat counterfeit products. By leveraging securely generated verification hashes and dynamic QR codes, brands can ensure their products' authenticity at every step of the supply chain.

The ecosystem empowers brands and administrators through a perfectly synchronized web application featuring two main portals:
1. 🛡️ **Brand Admin Space**: A powerful, distraction-free dashboard where brands can register new product batches, download printable QR codes, and monitor live scan activities.
2. 👨‍⚖️ **Super Admin Portal**: A centralized management layer for platform administrators to oversee brand registrations and ensure system integrity.

---

## ✨ Key Features

### 🛡️ For Brands & Manufacturers
- **Instant Batch Generation**: Register new products and instantly generate secure cryptographic verification hashes.
- **Printable QR Codes**: Automatically generate downloadable PDF sheets containing scan-ready QR codes for your product packaging.
- **Live Scan Analytics**: Monitor product scans in real-time. View beautiful, responsive charts detailing:
  - **Status Breakdown**: Compare Genuine vs. Counterfeit scan attempts.
  - **Scans Over Time**: Track scanning trends on a daily basis.
  - **Device Fingerprints**: Group scan attempts by unique device characteristics.
  - **Geographic Activity**: Pinpoint exactly where in the world your products are being scanned.
- **Gamified, Professional Interface**: Navigate through a premium UI featuring deep teal and vibrant orange brand colors, glassmorphism, and smooth micro-animations.

### 🔍 For Consumers
- **Seamless Verification**: Consumers simply scan the QR code on a product to instantly verify if it is Genuine or a Counterfeit.
- **Detailed Product Info**: Upon a successful scan, consumers view rich product details, manufacturing information, and expiry dates.

---

## 🛠️ Technical Highlights
- **Beautiful UI/UX**: Designed with a premium aesthetic, custom CSS architectures, responsive flexbox/grid layouts, and interactive hovering states.
- **Data Visualization**: Integrated with **Chart.js** for rendering rich, dynamic analytics based on live MongoDB aggregations.
- **Robust Backend Architecture**: Built on **Node.js/Express**, utilizing **Mongoose** for complex schema validation and efficient database querying.
